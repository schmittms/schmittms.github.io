Horowitz Improvising, etc.

Why does improvisation seem so rare among professional pianists? If I recall correctly, cadenzas used to be totally improvised. Even after that practice largely stopped in favor of prewritten cadenzas, there are famous anecdotes of Liszt improvising in concerts, or Schumann improvising "vignettes" of his friends at parties. I am sure many pianists today can also improvise (though maybe not quite like Horowitz...). I wish they would!

This recording also has some funny exchanges between Horowitz and the sound engineer.

Erlkönig Sand Art
